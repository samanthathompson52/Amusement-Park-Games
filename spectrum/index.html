How to play
  1. Pass the phone to the Psychic. Only they look — they see a hidden target zone on the spectrum.
  2. The Psychic says one clue out loud that points to the zone (no touching the dial).
  3. Everyone else discusses and drags the needle to where they think it is.
  4. Score by how close you land: bullseye 4, then 3, 2, or 0.
  5. Teams: the other team then bets Left/Right of the guess for a bonus point.
