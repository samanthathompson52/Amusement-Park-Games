How to play
  1. Sit in a circle with one phone. Tap Start.
  2. Letters: say any word that contains the fragment shown (e.g. TR → "street"). Topics: name something that fits.
  3. As soon as you say a word, tap Pass and hand the phone to the next player.
  4. The bomb has a hidden fuse — when it blows, whoever's holding the phone loses. No repeating words!
